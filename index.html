About Me

It all started when my husband and I visited Tunisia for the first time together. He fell in love with all the colors, the history, and the uniqueness of the Tunisian artisanal handmade products. It was during his first visit to the "Medina" (an old souk) that he decided that it would be a great idea to bring all this vibe to the United States. Being of Tunisian origin myself, I know all about the hard work and passion that the craftsmen pour into their work to get such unique products. That knowledge motivated me to make the move and proudly start the journey of "Cedar Gallery."

-Wafa "Alex" El Badewi
